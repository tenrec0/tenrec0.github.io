Tenrec: Tencent Dataset

A Large-Scale Multipurpose Benchmark Dataset for Recommender Systems

About Tenrec

Tenrec is a large-scale benchmark dataset for recommeder systems. It was collected from two different feeds recommendation platforms of Tencent. Our aim is to solve the problem that it is difficult to adequately test the performance of the model due to the small size of the current recommendation system dataset or the single content of the dataset.

We first randomly draw around 5.02 million users from the QK-video database; then, we extract their feedback (around 493 million), including both positive feedback (i.e. video click, share, like and follow) and negative feedback (with exposure but no user action); finally, we obtain around 142 million clicks, 10 million likes, 1 million shares and 0.86 million follows, alongside 3.75 million videos. Besides, there are anonymized age and gender features for users, and the video type feature for items. We perform similar data extraction strategy for QK-article, QB-video, and QB-article.:

If you are interested in using this dataset in your research work, welcome to cite this paper:

Guanghu Yuan, Fajie Yuan, Yudong Li, Beibei Kong, Shujie Li, Lei Chen, Min Yang, Chenyun Yu, Bo Hu, Zang Li, Yu Xu, Xiaohu Qie. Tenrec: A Large-Scale Multipurpose Benchmark Dataset for Recommender Systems NIPS 2022.
